What to expect from Metaverse?

The Metaverse is a permanent and persistent multiuser environment that combines physical reality with digital virtuality. It is the post-reality cosmos. It is built on the convergence of technologies, such as virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality, that allow for multimodal interactions with digital objects, virtual surroundings, and people (AR). As a result, the Metaverse is a permanent multiuser platform that connects a network of socially interactive, networked immersive worlds.

The Metaverse is built on technologies that allow for multimodal interactions with digital things, people, and virtual surroundings. Stereoscopic displays that can transmit the sense of depth enable the representational quality of the XR system. This is feasible because to separate displays for each eye that are somewhat different and mimic real-world settings. High-resolution XR screens enable a broad user field of vision that can range from 90 to 180 degrees. Compared to 2D systems, XR systems also provide greater aural experiences. The creation of soundscapes that notably improve immersion in AR and VR is made possible by 3D, spatial, or binaural audio.
